Child Care Courses

We offer a huge range of childcare courses and qualifications all designed to be studied online or with a study pack from home. Leading a busy life shouldn’t mean you miss out on the opportunity to learn, develop your career, and achieve your goals. Whether you’re juggling a job, looking after a family, or have other life commitments, with our range of distance learning courses you can study at your own pace, in your own time. Including courses in early years, child psychology, and teaching, here you’ll find out everything you need to know.

Our early years courses are ideal for those wanting to work with children from birth up to the age of five. These courses cover children and are ideal for those wanting to work in early years settings such as nurseries and preschools, as well as those looking to become a childminder or nanny.

KidsMinds Activites

Enjoy all the amazing things to do in Watford and have a magical family time any time of the year!

Parents Paradise may sound more like a spa than a soft play, however if the children are happy and entertained then parents can relax! Right in the centre of Watford this multi-level play frame even has a go-karting track and a shooting area.

Meet friendly animals at Farmyard Funworld or go for a walk through ancient woodland at Whippendell Woods.

On this Watford family day out head travel a play frame, ride the dodgems and there’s a special sensory area for toddlers.
