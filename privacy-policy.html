All early years providers are required by the EYFS and Ofsted to have policies and procedures in place to protect the children in their care.

Template policies and procedures are provided for all facets of the EYFS in the Alliance document Essential Policies & Procedures for the EYFS, including safeguarding and child protection.

When it's time to write the rules and procedures for your setting, you should take the following into account:

  • What are the possible safeguarding concerns with this provision?
  • How should everyone react in this situation?
  • What are the specifications?
