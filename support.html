So who's behind the KIDSMINDS

We’re a dedicated, small and enthusiastic team coming together from a variety of backgrounds; teaching, technology, event management, digital hypermedia storytelling and the civil service, to name a few! Most of us are based in Lewes, East Sussex.

We're here to help!(advice)

We aim to make Tapestry as intuitive as possible, but with so many options and features, you might need a bit of help sometimes.

This page is dedicated to linking you to all our support resources. We’ve tried to make things available in different formats so you can find help in the way that suits you best.

If you can’t find what you’re looking for, or you’d just prefer to ask someone, get in touch with our product support team – we’re always happy to help! Just email customer.service@kidsminds.uk with your question. We aim to reply to support requests within 1 working day, but it might take us a little longer if it’s an especially busy time of year.

If you use social media, you can find us on Facebook , Twitter , Instagram , and LinkedIn .
