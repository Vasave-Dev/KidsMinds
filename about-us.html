Who we are? Our Story

Kids Minds was founded by experienced childcare providers with a vision to provide comprehensive and personalized childcare.

We prioritise the individual needs of each child, ensuring their safety, happiness, and educational growth. With a commitment to excellence, we create a warm environment for children to explore, learn, and build lasting friendships. Open communication, collaboration with parents, and ongoing professional development are at the core of our approach.

We take pride in being a trusted partner for families, offering peace of mind and exceptional care. Join us as we nurture the next generation in a supportive community.

Why Kids Minds

We are an Ofsted-certified childminder agency with awards. In order to set up your new childcare business and help you become a licenced childminder or early educator, we offer subsidised start-up training. We can also assist you in increasing your visibility and locating local parents who are looking for childcare.

Additionally, you will continue to get training and support as a part of our network of qualified childminders. We provide several start-up options to meet the preferred amount of assistance you require to launch and manage your own childcare business from home.

To lessen the burden of running your own firm, our full support services also include regular business and marketing support.
